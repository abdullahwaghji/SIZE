About Moon

Established in 2008, Moon Textiles is a modern fabric manufacturing & printing company headquartered in Karachi, Pakistan. The company specializes in developing an extensive range of woven and knit fabrics for the apparel industry. All our fabric is manufactured on demand. Apart from Global retail brands and their respective converters, we cater to garment manufacturers and private entities who are looking to source high quality finished fabric. After a decade of service, the Family at Baraka Textiles grows stronger with each day. Our mill employs over 300 people who have shown exemplary skill and helped the company export over 50 million meters of fabric to date. With over 1000 unique shipments done, our shared experience helps us provide our clients with customised fabric of exceptional quality.
